Idiom Privacy policy

IdiomDictionary respects and protects the privacy of all users who use the service. In order to provide you with more accurate and personalized services, IdiomDictionary will use and disclose your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy. But we will treat this information with a high degree of diligence and prudence. Except as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy, we will not disclose or provide this information to a third party without your prior permission. We will update this privacy policy from time to time. When you agree to our service usage agreement, you are deemed to have agreed to the full content of this privacy policy. This privacy policy is an integral part of our service utilization agreement.

  1. application scope

A) when you use IdiomDictionary account, you verify personal identity information according to IdiomDictionary request.

B) When you use the IdiomDictionary network service, IdiomDictionary automatically receives and records information on your mobile phone, including but not limited to your IP address, browser type, language used, access date and time, software and hardware features, and web page records of your needs;

The use of information 2.

A) After you get your data, IdiomDictionary uploads it to the server to generate your ranking data so that you can better use the service.

  1. information disclosure

A) IdiomDictionary will not disclose your information to an untrusted third party.

B) Disclosure to a third party or an administrative or judicial institution in accordance with the relevant provisions of the law or the requirements of the administrative or judicial organs;

C) If you violate relevant Chinese laws, regulations or IdiomDictionary service agreements or relevant rules, you need to disclose to a third party;

  1. information storage and exchange

The information and information about you collected by IdiomDictionary will be stored on the servers of IdiomDictionary and/or its affiliated companies, and may be transmitted to your country, region or IdiomDictionary outside the country where the information and information is collected and accessed, stored and displayed abroad.

  1. information security

A) IdiomDictionary account has security protection function. Please keep your user name and password information properly. IdiomDictionary By encrypting user passwords and other security measures to ensure that your information is not lost, not abused and changed. Despite the aforementioned security measures, please also note that there is no “perfect security measures” on the information network.

隐私政策

1.适用范围

a)在您使用掌上查询助手帐号时，您根据掌上查询助手要求进行个人身份信息验证；

b)在您使用掌上查询助手网络服务，掌上查询助手自动接收并记录的您的手机上的信息，包括但不限于您的IP地址、浏览器的类型、使用的语言、访问日期和时间、软硬件特征信息及您需求的网页记录等数据；

2.信息的使用

a)在获得您的数据之后，掌上查询助手会将其上传至服务器，以生成您的排行榜数据，以便您能够更好地使用服务。

3.信息披露

a)掌上查询助手不会将您的信息披露给不受信任的第三方。

b)根据法律的有关规定，或者行政或司法机构的要求，向第三方或者行政、司法机构披露；

c)如您出现违反中国有关法律、法规或者掌上查询助手服务协议或相关规则的情况，需要向第三方披露；

4.信息存储和交换

掌上查询助手收集的有关您的信息和资料将保存在掌上查询助手及（或）其关联公司的服务器上，这些信息和资料可能传送至您所在国家、地区或掌上查询助手收集信息和资料所在地的境外并在境外被访问、存储和展示。

5.信息安全

a)掌上查询助手帐号均有安全保护功能，请妥善保管您的用户名及密码信息。掌上查询助手通过对用户密码进行加密等安全措施确保您的信息不丢失，不被滥用和变造。尽管有前述安全措施，但同时也请您注意在信息网络上不存在“完善的安全措施”。
